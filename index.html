PAPER SUBMISSION

Papers submitted to INTERNATIONAL CONCLAVE ON SUSTAINNOVATION 1.0 (ICS-1.0) should contain original research/study and should not have been published or submitted for publication elsewhere. Papers will be reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarised material The IEEE anti-plagiarism policy is applicable to all submissions.
